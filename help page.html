Orders and Shipping

  • How do I place an order?
    Browse our collection of books, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout when ready.
  • How can I track my order?
    Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order through our website or the courier's website.
  • What shipping options are available?
    We offer standard and expedited shipping. Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your location.
  • How can I change or cancel my order?
    Orders can be modified or canceled within 24 hours of purchase. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Returns and Refunds

  • What is your return policy?
    We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The book must be in its original condition.
  • How do I return an item?
    Log into your account and go to "My Orders." Select the item you wish to return and follow the steps to initiate the return process.
  • When will I receive my refund?
    Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item.
